Welcome to Glenstal Cricket Club

​Est. 2025 | Limerick, Ireland

​Born out of a passion to meet the growing demand for cricket among Limerick's international students, professionals and cricket enthusiasts. Glenstal Cricket Club has quickly grown from a simple idea into a thriving sporting community. Securing a historic home ground lease at Glenstal Abbey in August 2025, we proudly affiliate with the Munster Cricket Union (MCU) to bring competitive and inclusive cricket to the region.

History

Every great sports club begins with a shared vision. The concept of Glenstal Cricket Club was conceived in 2024 to address the rapidly growing demand for cricket within the Limerick community. Driven by an influx of international students and foreign professionals across regional universities, healthcare sectors, and multinational corporations, a dedicated group of students initially advocated for dedicated cricket facilities at the University of Limerick (UL). When UL confirmed in August 2024 that campus constraints prevented the development of a new pitch, Renjith, Jijo, and Jino pivoted toward establishing a private cricket ground. Joined soon after by Karan, the team launched a strategic search for a suitable venue. The defining breakthrough came in April 2025, when Sarah Fitzgerald recommended contacting Glenstal Abbey. By August 2025, Br. Padraig formally approved the proposal and executed the lease, securing a permanent home ground and cementing the club's strategic direction. The name "Glenstal Cricket Club" was finalized in November 2025, concurrent with the commencement of the Munster Cricket Union (MCU) affiliation process. ​The momentum carried swiftly into 2026. By March, the club registered 50 paid members, demonstrating remarkable early-stage growth. On April 12, 2026, Glenstal Cricket Club played its inaugural official match against Limerick Cricket Club in Adare.

This milestone was followed on April 27, 2026, by the official inauguration of the Glenstal Abbey ground, establishing a permanent base of operations. Today, Glenstal CC continues to build its organizational structure, expand its membership base, and actively participate in regional competitions. The administration remains focused on driving sustainable infrastructure, fostering broad community participation, and preparing for comprehensive competitive integration in future seasons.

Player development sits at the core of Glenstal Cricket Club’s sporting ethos. To accelerate skill acquisition and provide valuable match experience, the club proudly launched the Glenstal Premier League (GPL) in 2026 The primary objective of the GPL is to bridge the gap for amateur cricketers, particularly those transitioning from soft-ball formats to competitive hard-ball cricket. The league places a strong emphasis on mentoring students and young athletes, providing a structured pathway into senior competitive cricket. The inaugural season of the GPL commenced on May 16, 2026, featuring a robust six-team tournament structure designed to elevate regional cricket standards and optimize Glenstal CC's readiness for full integration into Munster Cricket Union competitions 2027 season.

What We Do

​The Glenstal Premier League (GPL): Launched in May 2026, our 6-team league bridges the gap for amateur, student, and soft-ball players transitioning to competitive hard-ball cricket. ​MCU Competitions: We actively compete in the MCU T20 Tournament and the Minor Cup, alongside hosting thrilling friendly fixtures with regional clubs. ​Community & Safety: Led by certified coaches and Irish Red Cross first-aiders, we prioritize player development, safety, and welfare above all.

Looking to the Future

We are a club built on diversity and long-term sustainability. Our immediate goals for next season include: ​Expanding Participation: Launching a brand-new Women’s team and a Youth Academy. ​Growing Our Roster: Increasing paid membership to over 75+ members. ​Going Premier: Fielding 3 to 4 teams across the MCU Premier Division down to Division 4. ​Join the Journey. Whether you are an experienced player, a student looking to play, or an amateur eager to learn, there is a place for you at Glenstal CC.
